Realme (stylized as realme) is a brand of Android based smartphones developed by Realme Chongqing Mobile Telecommunications Corporation, Ltd., a manufacturer based in Shenzhen, China. It was founded by Li Bingzhong (known as "Sky" Li) on May 4, 2018, who was former vice president of Oppo. Started originally as a sub-brand of Oppo, Realme eventually ventured as its own brand. Realme became the fastest-growing 5G smartphone brand in Q3 2021 with an 831% growth rate. Product By Realme
